Selenium Webdriver

Scrapping with BeautifulSoup is effective, but we can't do everything we might want to, like click buttons or type into input fields or scroll.

Selenium will let us mimic all of that to interact and communicate with the browser.

We might us it to automate a bunch of tasks that we don't want to do ourselves. S

This can end up being way faster because the browser is automatically sending a bunch of the headers and whatnot that we can't mimic as easily with requests and BeautifulSoup.
